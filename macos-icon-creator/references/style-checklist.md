# Style Checklist

Use this as the final self-check before returning an icon.

- The icon reads at 16, 32, 64, and 128 px without relying on tiny detail.
- The composition has one clear focal object.
- Foreground and background are balanced; the background is not doing all the work.
- Depth comes from planes, highlights, and overlap, not from random glow.
- Any highlight that remains has a material reason to exist; it is not a default top gloss.
- The squircle supports or fuses with the composition instead of acting like a blank badge.
- At least consider whether the icon benefits from overlap, edge pressure, or a small breakout rather than total containment.
- The result does not look like a pasted logo centered in a rounded square.
- There is no text, UI screenshot fragment, or decorative noise that weakens the silhouette.
- The palette has a clear hierarchy and does not depend on many unrelated colors.
